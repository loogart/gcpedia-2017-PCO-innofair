Virtual Tradeshow

The Trade Show will be showcasing the latest innovations from departments and agencies across the Public Service. The indoor component at 90 Elgin Street will showcase examples of excellence and innovation in how we support the government, as well as how we are building a healthy and productive workforce. The outdoor component along Sparks Street will focus on excellent service to Canadians.
